Setup Guide

To connect to an existing ADX cluster:

  1. Register an App Registration in Azure AD and copy the Tenant ID and Client ID.
  2. Create a Client Secret for the app registration.
  3. In ADX, grant the service principal viewer or ingestor permissions on the database.
  4. Enter your cluster URL in the form — e.g.
    https://mycluster.eastus.kusto.windows.net
  5. Click Test Connection to verify.

Settings saved here are stored in the local database and take effect immediately — no restart needed.
