Observer Guide

Read-only monitoring
As an Observer you have read-only access to the monitoring views below — perfect for watching how an event is going without the ability to change anything. You cannot start/stop the game, edit the scenario or configuration, manage users or teams, grade or adjust scores, or take backups. Your pages live in the Facilitator menu in the sidebar.
Analytics

A facilitator's-eye view of the event: solve rates by challenge and category, difficulty calibration (which challenges are too easy/hard), engagement over time, and ADX ingestion health. Use it to gauge pacing and spot challenges nobody can crack.

Open Analytics
Live Answer Feed

A near-real-time stream of every submission (correct and incorrect) as players work, with an integrity flags panel that surfaces possible shared answers, fast copies, or burst solving. These flags are advisory only — they never penalize anyone automatically.

Open Live Answer Feed
Run History

A log of each data-generation run: when it started and finished, how long it took, whether it succeeded / errored / was cancelled, the scenario window, and per-table ingested-row counts. Handy for confirming the data players are querying actually generated.

Open Run History
Audit Log

An append-only record of privileged admin actions — who did what, to which target, with a timestamp — filterable by category. Use it to understand what facilitators have changed during the event.

Open Audit Log
During an event — what to watch
  • Use Analytics to catch a challenge with a 0% solve rate early — it may be mis-worded.
  • Keep the Live Answer Feed open to feel the room's momentum and notice integrity flags.
  • If players report "no data," check Run History for a failed or cancelled run.
  • For a room display, open the big-screen scoreboard 🆕 — a full-screen live board with a first-blood / badge event ticker.
